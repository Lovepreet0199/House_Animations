How to Use the Snap Room

Click objects in the room to interact with them.

Window

Toggles day/night mode.

Lamp

Turns lighting on and off.

Books

The red and blue books on top-left shelf fall.

Camera

Freezes the scene like a snapshot with that shutter sound.

Cat Race

Starts a random animated race between two cats.

Cats

Click on the cat to hear them meow.

Reset Race

Resets cats to the start point.

Reset Frame

Restores everything to original state and plays animation.

Hover over Objects in room for animations

Picture Frame

Frame tilts as if it was falling from right side.

Books

Feel like you're selecting books.

Clock

Clock becomes bigger.

Lamp

Lamp tilts to the right.
